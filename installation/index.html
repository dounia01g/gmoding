GTA 5 Mobile Installation

Your complete step-by-step guide for Android & iOS devices.

Android Installation

  1. Download the .apk file.
  2. Locate the downloaded file and tap to begin installation.
  3. If prompted, you must enable "Install from unknown sources" in your device settings.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

iOS Installation Methods

What You'll Need (for most methods):

  • A Windows PC or macOS computer
  • A USB cable to connect your iPhone
  • Your Apple ID & password

  1. On a computer, install AltServer from altstore.io.
  2. Windows users must also install iTunes & iCloud directly from Apple's website.
  3. Connect your iPhone to your computer via USB.
  4. In the AltServer menu on your computer, select "Install AltStore," then choose your iPhone.
  5. Enter your Apple ID credentials when prompted.
  6. On your iPhone, go to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management and "Trust" your profile.
  7. Open AltStore on your iPhone, navigate to the 'My Apps' tab, tap the + icon, and select the downloaded .ipa file.
  8. Important: You must refresh your apps within AltStore at least once every 7 days by connecting to your computer.

  1. Download and install Sideloadly on your computer from sideloadly.io.
  2. Connect your iPhone and open the Sideloadly application.
  3. Drag the downloaded .ipa file into the Sideloadly window.
  4. Enter your Apple ID and click the “Start” button.
  5. After installation, navigate to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management on your device and "Trust" the new profile.

  1. Download Apple Configurator 2 from the Mac App Store.
  2. Connect your iPhone via USB.
  3. Open Apple Configurator 2, drag the .ipa file onto your device icon.
  4. Wait for the app to install.
  5. Trust the developer profile in Settings > General > VPN & Device Management.

  • Install a third-party app store (e.g., Scarlet, TrollStore, Esign) on your device.
  • Download the .ipa file directly onto your iPhone.
  • Use the "Share" or "Open In..." functionality to open the IPA file with your chosen third-party store.
  • Follow the store's prompts to sign and install the application.
  • You may need to "Trust" the store's developer profile in your device settings.
